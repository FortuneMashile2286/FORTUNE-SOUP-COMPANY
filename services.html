Our Services

At Fortune Soup Company, we do more than feed people — we restore hope and dignity. Our services aim to uplift communities, support families, and bring warmth to those who need it most.

Community Soup Kitchens

We serve warm, nutritious soup to the homeless, vulnerable families, and individuals in need. Our kitchens offer not just food, but comfort and connection.

Event & Outreach Catering

We provide reliable soup catering for charity drives, school programs, community events, fundraisers, and outreach activities—bringing warmth to every gathering.

Soup Delivery Service

Affordable, home-style soup delivered directly to families and workers. Fresh, healthy, and perfect for busy lifestyles.

Partnership Programs

We collaborate with NGOs, churches, schools, and community groups to expand our reach. Together, we can feed more families and build stronger communities.

Adopt-a-Pot Donation Program

Individuals and businesses can sponsor a soup pot that feeds 40–60 people. Donors receive updates about the community served by their generosity.

Monthly Food Parcels

We prepare monthly food boxes containing essentials like vegetables, bread, and soup ingredients. These parcels help families stay nourished beyond our daily soup servings.
